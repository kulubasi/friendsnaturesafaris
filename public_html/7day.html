Package 2: Day 1: Entebbe Airport –Ngamba Island.

  1. Day 1: Kampala to Murchsion Falls National Park.

    Depending on the time you arrive you meet our guide at Entebbe International Airport and transfer to Entebbe Backpackers check in, then visit Ngamba Island for orphaned Chimpanzees after that return back to the Campsite for dinner and overnight at full board basis ,If you arrive late you spent a night in kampala at Natete Backpackers. • Driving time: ± 3 hours • Entebbe /Kampala Backpackers.

  2. Day 2: Entebbe/Kampala to Kibale Forest National Park.

    Early morning we will be picked up and driven by our guide/drive to Fort Portal district via Mubende. Making a stopover at Nakayima tree shrine believed to be hosting the spirit of Nakayima.we will have a lunch break before touching down late afternoon in Fort Portal. We continue to the park and check in at our lodge for Dinner and overnight stay. • Driving time: ± 6,5 hours • Camp site

  3. Day 3: Kibale Forest National Park.

    This day you will start searching for Chimpanzees and other primates including red tailed and grey cheek Mangabey and other primates, the guide will provide detailed explanations not only on primates but also the forest fauna and flora and will ensure that your walk is both informative and enjoyable. Afternoon visit to Bigodi. This walk is excellent for viewing bird life. • Camp site

  4. Day 4: Kibale Forest NP To Queen Elizabeth NP.

    After break fast ,Proceed to queen Elizabeth National park following the mystical Rwenzori Mt. Range , a game drive en route to view Lion, Elephant, Leopard, Buffalo, Hyena and a variety of Antelope species. Enjoy the unique 2 hour boat ride on the Kazinga Channel. You will have a chance to view Hippos on close range and other bird species Dinner and overnight at Mweya Campsite

  5. Day 5: Queen Elizabeth National Park.

    After break fast ,Proceed to queen Elizabeth National park following the mystical Rwenzori Mt. Range , a game drive en route to view Lion, Elephant, Leopard, Buffalo, Hyena and a variety of Antelope species. Enjoy the unique 2 hour boat ride on the Kazinga Channel. You will have a chance to view Hippos on close range and other bird species Dinner and overnight at Mweya Campsite

  6. Day 6: Queen Elizabeth NP to L.Mburo NP

    Leave Queen Elizabeth to Lake Mburo National park with a stop over at Mbarara town for lunch. After lunch, proceed to the park with game drive en-route. Evening, nature walk in the savannah park is amazing you should also know that Lake Mburo is the only park in Uganda where you can carry out a safari on foot so many animals can be spotted with accompany of an armed guide. Overnight at Rwonyo Rest Campsite.

  7. Day 7: Mburo-Entebbe.

    Depending on your flight, you can choose to go for a morning boat cruise otherwise transfer to the airport to catch your flight your flight back home.

What You Carry In your daypack you will need to carry extra warm clothing, a rain jacket, water bottle, camera gear, valuables and personal items. For detailed and more tailor made itineraries, Send us an email on: book@friendsnaturesafaris.net
